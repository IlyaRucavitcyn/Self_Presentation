# Market Research — Aggregation Platforms

## Methodology

Salary data collected from public aggregation platforms and job market analysis sources. All figures are in EUR, monthly gross. Data is filtered to B2B contract where confirmed; sources that do not distinguish contract type are noted.

---

## Table 1 — Salary Ranges

| Role | Source | Market Range (EUR/mo) |
|---|---|---|
| Engineering Manager | Itentio — 38 postings, B2B, Poland (2025) | 7,440 - 9,490 |
| Engineering Manager | Glassdoor — Warsaw, P25-P75 | 6,200 - 9,460 |

---

## Table 2 — Bonus Norms

| Metric | Source | Market Standard |
|---|---|---|
| Tech sector bonus | Oyster HR (2025) | 10-20% of base annually |
| Leadership bonus | Oyster HR (2025) | 10-30% of base annually |
| EM bonus prevalence | WorldSalaries | 76% receive bonus |

**Note:** Bonus is typically on top of the advertised base salary, not included in the stated ranges.
