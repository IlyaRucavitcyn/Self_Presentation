## Key Observations

1. Roles combining pre-sales, multi-department oversight, or 30+ team leadership (#7, #11, #14) consistently command 8,300-11,300 EUR/mo — the top tier in the dataset.
2. Medical insurance and sports (Multisport) are standard benefits, provided on top of stated salary.
3. Where vacation is disclosed, 25-26 days is the norm (Tidio, vacancy #6).
4. Bonus is typically on top of the stated salary range — only 3 of 14 vacancies disclose details (#3: company stock, #5: yearly bonus, #8: annual bonus + equity).
