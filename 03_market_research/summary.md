# Market Research — Summary

## Data Sources

This research combines two complementary approaches:

1. **Aggregation platforms** — Glassdoor, Itentio (38 B2B postings), Oyster HR, WorldSalaries — providing salary ranges, bonus norms, and benefits standards for Engineering Manager and Director of Engineering roles in Poland.
2. **Active vacancies** — 14 B2B job postings from JustJoinIT, NoFluffJobs, BulldogJob, and LinkedIn, collected in February 2026 — providing real, current market rates with role scope details.

Full data: [aggregated_market_data.md](aggregated_market_data.md) | [local_boards.md](local_boards.md)

---

## Market Salary Range — Engineering Manager (B2B, Poland)

| Source | Range (EUR/mo) |
|---|---|
| Itentio — 38 B2B postings (2025) | 7,440 - 9,490 |
| Glassdoor — EM, Warsaw, P25-P75 | 6,200 - 9,460 |
| Active vacancies — median band (14 postings) | 6,400 - 8,700 |
| Active vacancies — full range | 5,083 - 11,295 |

**Consolidated market band for a standard Engineering Manager (B2B):** approximately **6,400 - 9,500 EUR/mo**, with Director/Head of Engineering roles reaching 11,000+.

## Bonus Norms

| Metric | Market Standard |
|---|---|
| Bonus prevalence among EMs | 76% receive bonus (WorldSalaries) |
| Tech sector bonus range | 10-20% of base annually (Oyster HR) |
| Leadership bonus range | 10-30% of base annually (Oyster HR) |
| Equity/stock | Offered by some (ServiceTitan, others) |

Bonus is generally on top of the advertised salary range — most vacancies do not include bonus in the stated figures.

## Role Scope in Market Postings

The 14 active vacancies reveal a clear pattern — scope and compensation scale together:

| Scope Tier | Typical Team | Typical Responsibilities | Salary Band (EUR/mo) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Standard EM** | 5-15 people, 1-2 teams | People management, delivery, mentoring | 5,000 - 7,800 |
| **Senior EM** | 15-25 people, 2-4 teams | Cross-team alignment, coaching tech leads, hiring | 6,400 - 9,500 |
| **Head of Engineering** | 20-40+ people, 4-6 leads | Engineering org leadership, pre-sales, strategy, C-level collaboration | 8,300 - 11,300 |

Roles that combine engineering leadership with pre-sales, business development, or multi-department oversight consistently command the higher end of the range.

---

## Key Findings

1. **The standard EM market floor (B2B) is approximately 6,400 EUR/mo**, confirmed by both aggregation platforms and live vacancies.
2. **Benefits (medical, sports, equipment, 25-26 vacation days) are standard** across the market and are provided on top of salary, not as part of it.
3. **Role scope is the strongest differentiator** — managing 30+ people across multiple departments, combined with pre-sales and technology strategy responsibilities, aligns with Head of Engineering / Senior EM postings at 8,300-11,300 EUR/mo.
4. **Bonus (10-20%) is the norm**, but it is applied on top of market-rate base salaries.
