- **Total GPM across departments**: 772,120 EUR (2024) → 906,306 EUR (2025) — **+134,186 EUR (+17.4%)**

| Department | GPM% 2024 | GPM% 2025 |

All three departments improved margins year-over-year. Growth in both profitability and team size, demonstrating effective scaling without margin dilution.
