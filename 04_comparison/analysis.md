**Vacation clarification:** In the Polish B2B tech market, the advertised 25-26 vacation days are separate from the 13 public holidays — mirroring the employment contract (UoP) framework. Benefits are provided on top of salary in market postings, not instead of it.

## 6. Observations

1. Total compensation of 6,440 EUR/mo (base + bonus) is at the bottom of the standard EM range (6,400-8,700 EUR/mo), where typical scope is 5-15 people in a single department.

2. The current role scope — 31 people across 3 departments, pre-sales ownership (30%), technology strategy, hiring/salary decisions, cross-functional leadership — maps to the Head of Engineering tier (8,300-11,300 EUR/mo) in the collected market data.

3. Market bonus norms (10-20%) are applied on top of market-rate base salaries. The current bonus percentage is within range, but the base it is applied to is below market.

4. Standard market benefits (medical, sports, equipment) are not part of the current package. Market vacation standard is 25-26 days plus 13 public holidays (38-39 total). Current allowance is 19 days inclusive of public holidays taken.
