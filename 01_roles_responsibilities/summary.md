# Roles & Responsibilities

## Official Title
**Head of JS, PHP and QA Departments** — Sigli

## Scope
- 3 departments: JavaScript, PHP, Quality Assurance
- 31 people under management
- Company size: ~100 people worldwide

## Functional Roles Covered

The official title encompasses responsibilities that the industry typically distributes across 3-4 separate roles:

### 1. Pre-sales Engineer — ~30% of time
- Initial project validation and analysis
- Forming pre-sales project teams (excluding sales personnel)
- Team coordination during pre-sales phase
- Estimation oversight and implementation
- Writing delivery proposals (key project information documents)
- Project budgeting
- Client presentations when necessary

### 2. VP of Engineering — ~25% of time
- Introducing new technologies and services (Salesforce, Odoo, low-code platforms)
- Leading internal initiatives: AI for Development, AI for QA
- Technology stack evolution and modernization

### 3. Resource Manager — ~25% of time
- Creating positions based on project needs
- Organizing technical interviews (including interviewer training)
- Making hiring decisions and offers
- Assigning developers to mentors
- Mentoring the mentors
- Salary decisions and negotiations
- HR issues (in collaboration with HR team)
- Handling escalated situations that mentors couldn't resolve
- Terminations when necessary

### 4. Project & Client Management — ~20% of time
- JS consulting
- PO/PM role on internal projects
- Discovery phase coordination and technical management

## Key Stakeholders
- CTO (direct manager and mentor)
- CDO (Delivery) and Delivery Managers
- Head of People and Culture
- TA Team (Talent Acquisition)
- Sales Development Team and Sales Operations
- Mentors from managed departments
- Developers from managed departments
- Clients

## Key Narrative Point
A single role that combines strategic engineering leadership, people management, pre-sales technical work, and hands-on project delivery — responsibilities that in most companies of similar size would be distributed across multiple dedicated positions.
